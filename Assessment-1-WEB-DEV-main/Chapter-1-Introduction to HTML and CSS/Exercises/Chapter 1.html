Café Mocha

This rich and indulgent café mocha recipe combines the bold flavor of espresso with the creamy sweetness of chocolate. Perfect for a cozy morning treat or an afternoon pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 cup of steamed milk
  • 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings (optional)

Method

  1. Brew one shot of espresso using your preferred method.
  2. Steam one cup of milk until frothy.
  3. In a mug, add the brewed espresso.
  4. Pour the steamed milk into the mug, mixing with the espresso.
  5. Add two tablespoons of chocolate syrup to the mug.
  6. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
  7. If desired, top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
